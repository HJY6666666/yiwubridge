# YiwuGo Agent Google Ads Launch Plan

Website: https://yiwugoagent.com/

## Campaign Type

Start with Search campaigns only. Do not start Display or Performance Max until the account has conversion data.

## Conversion Events

GA4 is installed with measurement ID `G-27W27BG511`.

The website now sends these lead events:

- `generate_lead`: contact form submit
- `whatsapp_click`: WhatsApp link click

In GA4, mark both events as Key events. Then link GA4 to Google Ads and import these events as conversions.

## Recommended Campaign Structure

Campaign: Search - Yiwu Sourcing Leads

Ad groups:

- Yiwu Sourcing Agent
- China Sourcing Agent
- Yiwugo Buying Service
- Warehouse Consolidation
- Amazon FBA and Ecommerce Sourcing

## Bidding

Start with manual CPC or Maximize clicks with a strict daily budget cap. Switch to Maximize conversions only after there is enough conversion data.

## Budget

Do not enable the campaign until the daily budget is confirmed by the owner.

Suggested test ranges:

- Conservative test: USD 10-15 per day
- Normal test: USD 20-30 per day
- Faster data test: USD 50 per day

## Locations

Start with English-speaking and high-intent importer markets:

- United States
- United Kingdom
- Canada
- Australia
- United Arab Emirates
- Saudi Arabia
- Singapore

Exclude China for buyer acquisition campaigns unless testing a separate remarketing or brand campaign.

## First 7-Day Optimization Rules

- Pause keywords with many clicks and no WhatsApp/form leads.
- Add search terms that are job seekers, free directories, courses, maps, or local consumer intent as negative keywords.
- Keep exact and phrase match tighter than broad match at the beginning.
- Review by country. Move expensive low-quality countries into a separate test or exclude them.
