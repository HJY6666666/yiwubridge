What buyers need

Quality control should create visible evidence

The best sourcing and fulfillment pages use QC as a trust builder: inspect before shipment, document defects, protect packaging, and give buyers enough evidence to approve, rework, or stop an order.

01

Sample confirmation

Check color, material, size, function, logo, packaging, and buyer-approved reference before mass order.

02

Finished goods check

Review quantity, appearance, mixed styles, carton marks, packing condition, and common visible defects.

03

Evidence and decision

Send photos, videos, inspection notes, and a clear recommendation: approve, rework, sort, or hold shipment.

Inspection points

What we can check before export

Product match

Confirm the received goods match your photo, sample, specification, color, size, and variant requirements.

Quantity and assortment

Check carton quantity, SKU mix, model numbers, color ratios, and obvious missing or wrong items.

Packaging and labels

Review inner packing, cartons, barcodes, FBA labels, shipping marks, inserts, and private label details.

Issue handling

If defects appear, we coordinate sorting, replacement, repacking, supplier rework, or holding goods before shipping.
