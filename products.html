Home storage and organization

Yiwu Market is especially strong for home storage and organization products. For new brands, we can compare materials, confirm carton size, negotiate lower MOQ, and check packaging before shipment.

Kitchenware

Kitchenware buyers can source stainless steel cookware, silicone utensils, lunch boxes, cutting boards, food containers, strainers, and small kitchen gadgets from Yiwu and nearby factory clusters. Household daily goods

Yiwu is a practical market for daily-use household goods such as cleaning brushes, microfiber cloths, plastic buckets, laundry racks, trash bins, slippers, hangers, and bathroom accessories. Shoes and hats

Yiwu offers many lightweight footwear and hat options, including slippers, sandals, caps, knitted hats, sun hats, and seasonal accessories. Fashion accessories

Fashion accessories are one of Yiwu's strongest wholesale categories. We can source jewelry, hair clips, sunglasses, belts, scarves, keychains, wallets, and small gift accessories. Home textiles
